Biography

I am an experienced developer transitioning to full stack web development. My prior experience was in mainframe development in the areas of finance, manufacturing, and retail.

My last position was at DST Systems as a Database Consultant. The primary goal of the position was to act as an advocate for the relational database, DB2. I mentored developers in the best practices of saving and querying data in the database. My group was responsible to monitor the performance of the various client systems and develop recommendations to improve program performance. Our recommendations included SQL coding, COBOL coding, and integrating the languages efficiently. There were many other duties including administering a small test database.

While at DST, I wrote several REXX programs to assist development. There are two notable programs--one scans through hundreds of libraries to find all the occurrences of a program and the other identified deprecated and non-standard practices.

Before becoming a Database Consultant, I was a senior developer leading a small team to enhance the finance system to include functions from a new client. Projects included splitting purchases into multiple accounts, special handling for low balance accounts, and splitting output files so they could be sent to two different print vendors.
